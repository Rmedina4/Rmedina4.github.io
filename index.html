About Me...

Hello, my name is Rudy Medina Jr. I'm currently a graduating senior at Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi finishing up a double major degree in computer science and mechanical engineering. So now your probably wondering "Why did he do a double major in MEEN and COSC?" Well... I graduated from a STEM-based high school that gave me exposure to many fields of engineering (civil, environmental, industrial, mechanical, and computer science). During my senior year of high school, I always knew I wanted to major in mechanical engineering, but I took one course in computer science that changed my future. This course wasn't strictly a class where I was going to learn just programming and compilers, but I was going to need to know the past knowledge that I had acquired in my mechanical engineering courses. This course was a robotics course where I used all my mechanical engineering skills in high school to create a robot from scratch and than went on to compete in many Texas Robotics competitions. The building of this robot was the no-brainer, but I fell in love with the programming aspect of making what I built move. From this day on I knew I had to attend college an pursue a double major in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science. Fast forward to today I kept my goal and have had the opportunity to intern in both mechanical engineering and computer science roles in the Texas area as well as the Silicon Valley area. Over the past 5 and 1/2 years with both my degrees I have gained experience in Mechanical System Design, Product Development, Robotics, System Administration, Software/Automation tools, and Cyber Security.
